Why IRIS?

At IRIS, we understand that terms and conditions can be lengthy, complex, and often difficult to understand. We believe that everyone should have the right to know exactly what they are agreeing to when using a software application or website, without sacrificing their privacy and security. That's why we created IRIS, a web-based application powered by advanced AI technology, to provide users with a clear and easy-to-understand summary of the key sections of terms and conditions agreements. Our solution allows users to make informed decisions about the policies they are agreeing to, increasing transparency and awareness around data privacy and security. At IRIS, we are dedicated to creating a safer and more secure online environment for everyone.
