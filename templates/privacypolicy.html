Privacy Policy
At IRIS, we understand that your privacy is important. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we use and protect that information.


Collection and Use of Information

IRIS does not collect any personal information from users. We only require users to input the terms and conditions of a software application or website for analysis. We use the ChatGPT API to analyze the policies and identify any concerning sections. The analysis results are presented to the user in a clear and easy-to-understand summary.


Storage and Protection of Information

We do not store any user data, including the terms and conditions entered into our application. Once the analysis is complete, the information is discarded. We take all reasonable steps to ensure the security of our users' information. We use industry- standard security measures to protect against the loss, misuse, or alteration of information under our control.


Third-Party Disclosure

IRIS does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties any user data.


Updates to Privacy Policy

IRIS reserves the right to modify this privacy policy at any time without notice. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically for any changes.


Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at support @irissgce.in.
